Shell Collector

Tutorial


  1. You'll start the game with a random number."Number To Get"
  2. There are four shells below.
    • Blue Shell
    • Green Shell
    • Red Shell
    • Gold Shell
  3. By clicking on a shell, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. To win the game you must match your number to the random number that is generated for you.
  5. If your total goes over the random number, you lose.
  6. The value of each shell will remain hidden until you click on them and will change each game.
